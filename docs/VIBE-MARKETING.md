# Vibe Marketing Playbook — Key Takeaways

Source: https://www.thevibemarketer.com/vibe-marketing-playbook.html

## Core Philosophy

**"Boring is differentiated."** Everyone sells hype. We sell compound growth.

### 5 Core Insights

1. **Research Is Everything** — Cast wide net, gather deep context (30-60 min minimum)
2. **Human Taste Is The Differentiator** — AI generates options, you pick winners
3. **Speed Through Systems** — Right frameworks = zero to shipped in hours
4. **Boring Is Differentiated** — Confidently boring compound growth wins
5. **Task-Based Agents Win** — Multiple expert perspectives (SEO expert, conversion expert, etc.)

## The 5-Stage Build Sequence

### Stage 1: Research (Deep Context)
- Market landscape & competitors
- Competitor screenshots & positioning analysis
- Customer language (actual words they use)
- Keyword validation

### Stage 2: Foundation (Voice + Positioning)
- Brand voice extraction
- 3-5 positioning angles
- Anti-positioning (what you're NOT)

### Stage 3: Structure (Keywords + Pillars)
- Keyword opportunities (gaps competitors miss)
- Content pillars (organize into themes)
- Quick wins (low-hanging fruit for 60-90 days)

### Stage 4: Assets (Landing Pages, Emails, Content)
- Landing page copy + design
- Email sequences (welcome, nurture, conversion)
- Lead magnets

### Stage 5: Iteration (Reject Until Right)
- "This is too generic. Make it more specific."
- Voice enforcement
- Quality gates: Would YOU buy from this page?

## The 8 Positioning Angles

| Angle | Description | When to Use |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Contrarian** | Challenge what everyone believes | Sophisticated market, strong opinions exist |
| **Unique Mechanism** | Lead with HOW, not just WHAT | Competitors have similar features |
| **Transformation** | Before/after, the gap closed | Emotional purchase, identity shift |
| **Enemy** | Position against common villain | Shared frustration exists |
| **Speed/Ease** | Compress time or reduce effort | Complex alternatives exist |
| **Specificity** | Hyper-specific about who/what | Crowded market, need differentiation |
| **Social Proof** | Lead with evidence, not claims | Have strong results to show |
| **Risk Reversal** | Make guarantee the headline | High perceived risk in purchase |

## SEO Principles

1. **Research first** — Find gaps vs competitors
2. **Programmatic opportunities** — Scalable patterns:
   - `[Service] for [industry]` → 50+ pages
   - `[Service] in [city]` → Local landing pages
   - `[Tool] vs [tool]` → Comparison pages
   - `Best [solution] for [use case]` → Use case pages
3. **Quality > quantity** — One excellent piece > five mediocre
4. **Human checkpoint** — Fact-check, add expertise, remove AI tells
5. **Don't publish all at once** — Velocity signals matter

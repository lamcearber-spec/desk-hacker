# Larry's TikTok/Instagram Playbook (Oliver Henry)

> Source: https://x.com/oliverhenry/status/2022011925903667547
> Saved: 2026-02-13

## Overview
Oliver Henry's OpenClaw agent "Larry" got **500K+ TikTok views in 5 days** promoting iOS apps (Snugly, Liply). $588 MRR, 108 paying subscribers.

## The Stack
- **OpenClaw** agent on old gaming PC (Ubuntu)
- **GPT image generation** (gpt-image-1.5) — ~$0.50/post, $0.25 with Batch API
- **Postiz** API — posts slideshows as TikTok drafts
- Human adds trending music + publishes (~60 seconds)

## The Format: Photo Carousels
TikTok data: slideshows get **2.9x more comments, 1.9x more likes, 2.6x more shares** vs video.
- 6 slides exactly (TikTok sweet spot)
- Text overlay on slide 1 with the hook
- Story-style caption mentioning app naturally
- Max 5 hashtags

## The Winning Hook Formula
> **[Person] + [conflict/doubt] → showed them AI → they changed their mind**

### Winners:
- "My landlord said I can't change anything so I showed her what AI thinks it could look like" → **234K views**
- "I showed my mum what AI thinks our living room could be" → **167K views**
- "My landlord wouldn't let me decorate until I showed her these" → **147K views**

### Losers:
- "Why does my flat look like a student loan" → 905 views
- "See your room in 12+ styles before you commit" → 879 views
- Feature-focused hooks = dead

## Key Lessons
1. **Skill files are everything** — 500+ lines teaching exact workflows
2. **Every failure becomes a rule, every success a formula**
3. **Lock image architecture** — same base across all slides, only change the style element
4. **Be obsessively specific** in image prompts (dimensions, window count, camera angle)
5. **Portrait orientation** (1024x1536) — landscape causes black bars, kills engagement
6. **Text overlay rules** — 6.5% font size minimum, position below status bar, limit line length
7. **"Before" images need signs of life** — mugs, remotes, everyday items (not empty rooms)
8. **Batch brainstorm 10-15 hooks** based on performance data, then schedule ahead
9. **Use OpenAI Batch API** for 50% cheaper overnight generation

## Adapting for DatevBereit
**Format:** Instagram carousels (same as TikTok slideshows)
**Hook formula adapted:**
- "Mein Steuerberater hat gesagt, meine Kontoauszüge sind ein Chaos — bis ich ihm DAS gezeigt habe"
- "Meine Buchhalterin wollte €500 für die DATEV-Konvertierung — dann hab ich ihr dieses Tool gezeigt"
- "3 Stunden manuelle Arbeit vs. 5 Minuten mit DatevBereit"

**Slides concept:**
1. Hook text on messy bank statement screenshot
2. Upload screen
3. Processing animation/screenshot
4. Clean DATEV export
5. Happy Steuerberater reaction / time saved
6. CTA: "Kostenlos testen → datev-bereit.de"

**Differences from Larry's setup:**
- B2B audience (Steuerberater, not consumers) — LinkedIn might outperform TikTok
- Less visual product — need creative before/after framing
- German market — hooks in German
- Instagram > TikTok for professional audience

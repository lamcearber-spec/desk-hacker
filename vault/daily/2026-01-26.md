## SEO Strategy (from @bloggersarvesh thread) — SAVE FOR SEO AGENT

**6-Step Local/Niche SEO Playbook:**

1. **Competitor Keyword Analysis**
   - Use Ahrefs to analyze competitor's top 20 pages
   - Extract target keywords, search volumes, difficulty scores
   - Prioritize into spreadsheet

2. **Low-Hanging Fruit Keywords**
   - Find high-intent keywords indicating ready-to-buy customers
   - For local: "near me", "emergency" keywords
   - For B2B SaaS: "[problem] lösung", "[tool] alternative", "wie [action]"

3. **Page Optimization**
   - Add relevant embeds (maps for local, tool integrations for SaaS)
   - Mention specific locations/niches naturally
   - Create checklist of updates

4. **Review/Social Proof Management**
   - Write keyword-rich responses to reviews
   - Keep friendly, mention services naturally
   - Fast response time improves rankings

5. **Content Feed Domination (GBP/Blog)**
   - Analyze competitor content gaps
   - Write high-impact posts with hard CTAs
   - Include niche-specific terms

6. **Competition Weakness Analysis**
   - Scan competitor sites for missing content
   - Find content gaps
   - Cover topics they miss → win #1 spot

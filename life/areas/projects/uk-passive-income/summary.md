# UK Passive Income Project

**For:** Arber's brother (moving to London)
**Goal:** Multiple passive revenue streams via web/mobile apps
**Structure:** UK Ltd (umbrella company) → multiple apps/products (same model as Radom UG)

**Constraints:**
- Must be simple to operate (not technical)
- UK/British market focus
- Low maintenance once running
- LLC setup is easy in UK (advantage over German UG)

**Strategy:**
- One Ltd, many revenue streams
- We build, he operates
- Start with 2-3 quick wins, expand from there

---

## Ideas Backlog

### 1. 🏠 Council Tax Band Checker / Appeal Helper
**Why UK:** Council tax bands are notoriously wrong. 400,000+ homes are in wrong bands.
**Model:** Free check → paid appeal letter generator (£20-50)
**Passive:** Template-based, minimal support needed
**Competition:** Low, mostly manual solicitors

### 2. 🚗 ULEZ / Clean Air Zone Checker
**Why UK:** ULEZ expanded in London, other cities adding zones
**Model:** Enter reg → instant check if car is compliant + what zones
**Monetization:** Affiliate to car sales, insurance, scrappage schemes
**Passive:** Data-driven, auto-updates

### 3. 📋 UK School Admissions Helper
**Why UK:** School applications are confusing, catchment areas complex
**Model:** Freemium - basic info free, detailed reports paid
**Timing:** Seasonal spikes (application deadlines)
**Passive:** Once data pipeline built, runs itself

### 4. 🧾 UK Tax Rebate Finder
**Why UK:** Millions eligible for rebates (work from home, uniforms, tools)
**Model:** Free check → take % of rebate or flat fee
**Passive:** HMRC has APIs, mostly automated
**Competition:** Exists but room for cleaner UX

### 5. 📦 UK Parcel Locker Finder
**Why UK:** Fragmented locker networks (Amazon, InPost, etc.)
**Model:** Unified search → affiliate/ads
**Passive:** Scrape/API data, minimal maintenance

### 6. 🏥 NHS Waiting Time Tracker
**Why UK:** NHS waiting times are a hot topic, data is public but scattered
**Model:** Free tool, monetize via health insurance affiliates
**Passive:** Data scraping + alerts

### 7. 🎓 Student Finance Calculator UK
**Why UK:** Confusing loan/grant system
**Model:** Calculator + tips → affiliate to student banks/cards
**Timing:** Yearly cycle around UCAS
**Passive:** Logic-based, rarely changes

### 8. 🏘️ Right to Buy Calculator
**Why UK:** Council tenants can buy at discount, complex eligibility
**Model:** Free calculator → connect with mortgage brokers (affiliate)
**Passive:** Simple form + rules engine

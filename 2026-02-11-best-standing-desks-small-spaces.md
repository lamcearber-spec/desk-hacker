Living in a studio apartment? Working from a closet-turned-office? You don't need a sprawling 72-inch desk to get the benefits of standing while working.

## What Makes a Great Small-Space Standing Desk?

Before diving into recommendations, here's what I prioritized:

| Feature | Why It Matters for Small Spaces |
|---------|--------------------------------|
| **Width under 40"** | Fits in alcoves, corners, and beside beds |
| **Depth 24"+** | Still fits a monitor + keyboard comfortably |
| **Cable management** | No room for cable spaghetti on the floor |
| **No bulky legs** | C-frame designs save precious inches |
| **Weight under 100 lbs** | Easier to move when rearranging |

## What About Manual/Crank Desks?

I skipped non-electric desks because:
- Price difference is minimal ($30-50)
- You're less likely to actually switch positions
- Small-space dwellers often multi-purpose rooms—ease matters

If budget is truly tight, consider a **desktop converter** instead.

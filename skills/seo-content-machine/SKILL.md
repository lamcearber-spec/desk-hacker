# SEO Content Machine

## Mission
Build profitable niche affiliate/info sites in the German accounting market. Revenue through affiliate commissions and display ads.

## Workflow

### 1. Keyword Research
- Target German long-tail keywords in accounting/bookkeeping/Steuerberater niche
- Focus on: low competition (KD < 30), high intent, 100-1000 monthly searches
- Tools: web_search, competitor analysis
- Output: keyword list in `vault/projects/seo-content-machine/keywords.md`

### 2. Site Planning
- Map keywords to pages (1 primary keyword per page)
- Create site structure (hub-and-spoke: pillar pages + supporting articles)
- Plan internal linking strategy
- Output: site map in `vault/projects/seo-content-machine/sitemap.md`

### 3. Content Creation
- Write in German (native quality, not translated)
- Format: problem → solution → CTA
- Include: H1-H3 structure, meta title/description, internal links
- Word count: 1,500-2,500 words per article
- Save articles to `vault/projects/seo-content-machine/content/`

### 4. Monetization
- Affiliate links: DATEV software, accounting tools, tax prep services
- Display ads: Once traffic > 1,000 sessions/month
- CTA to DatevBereit where relevant (cross-promotion)

## Rules
- No AI-detectable content — rewrite until natural
- No black hat SEO (no PBNs, no link schemes)
- Cite real sources, link to authoritative German sites
- Update vault after every work session

## Progress Tracking
Update `vault/projects/seo-content-machine/summary.md` after every session with:
- Keywords researched
- Content written
- Sites launched
- Traffic/revenue metrics

# Strategy: Research Services Agent

## Mission
To generate income through direct B2B sales of research services, focusing on cold email outreach and conversion.

## Current Focus
- Identifying B2B prospects in specific niches.
- Crafting personalized cold email campaigns.
- Delivering high-quality research on demand.

## Outreach Stages
1. **Prospect Identification:** Use web scraping (if available) or manual search to find relevant B2B contacts.
2. **Cold Email Batch 1:** Send initial outreach to a small, targeted group.
3. **Follow-up Sequence:** Implement a short follow-up sequence for non-responders.
4. **Lead Nurturing:** Engage with interested prospects to understand their needs and offer tailored research solutions.
5. **Conversion:** Close sales for research projects.

## Key Metrics
- Email open rates
- Reply rates
- Conversion rate (prospect to client)
- Average project value

# Lead Scraper Playbook

## Target: German SaaS Startups (50-500 employees)
High-value B2B prospects with budget for tools, services, and outsourcing. German market = less competition, higher willingness to pay.

## Data Sources:
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Free Trial)** - Filter by company size 50-500, industry "Software Development", Germany location. Export via manual scrape or PhantomBuster free tier.
2. ** Bundesverband IT-Mittelstand (BITMi)** - German IT SME association directory. Public member list with company details.
3. **Google Maps** - Search "Softwareentwicklung [city]" for local SaaS companies. Scrape business name, website, phone via Google Maps Scraper (free tier).

## Enrichment:
1. **Hunter.io (Free: 50 searches/mo)** - Domain-based email finder. Pattern: {firstname}@{domain} or use Email Finder tool.
2. **Apollo.io (Free: 50 credits/mo)** - Direct dials + verified emails. Best for finding decision-makers (CTO, Head of Engineering).

## Pricing:
- **$2 per lead** (name, company, verified email, LinkedIn URL)
- **$150 per 100 leads** (bulk discount)
- **$300 per 100 leads** WITH phone numbers + decision-maker titles

## Sales Channel:
**Fiverr Gig Strategy:**
- Gig Title: "I will find 100 verified B2B SaaS leads in Germany with email"
- Packages: Starter (50 leads/$99) → Pro (100 leads/$150) → Premium (100 leads + phone/$299)
- Keywords: "B2B lead generation", "German companies", "SaaS leads", "verified email"

**Direct Outreach (Alternative):**
- Target: Freelance sales reps, small B2B agencies on Upwork/Fiverr
- Message: "Need German SaaS leads? I deliver 100 verified contacts in 48h. $150 trial pack."

## First 100 Leads Plan:
1. **Day 1 (30 min):** Sign up Hunter.io + Apollo free tiers, set up LinkedIn Sales Navigator trial
2. **Day 1 (2 hrs):** Scrape BITMi member directory → collect 200 company names + websites
3. **Day 2 (2 hrs):** Run Hunter.io domain search on all 200 sites → extract ~80 emails
4. **Day 2 (2 hrs):** Apollo enrichment on remaining 120 for decision-makers → ~40 more leads
5. **Day 3 (1 hr):** Verify emails with free EmailVerifier tool, deduplicate, format CSV
6. **Day 3 (30 min):** Create Fiverr gig, post sample leads in gig gallery
7. **Day 4:** Launch gig, send 10 direct outreach messages on Upwork

**Goal:** First paying customer by Day 7.

# Strategy: Lead Scraper Agent

## Mission
To generate income by building and selling high-quality B2B lead lists, primarily for the German market.

## Current Focus
- Identifying target industries/niches for lead generation.
- Scraping public directories and sources for company data.
- Enriching lead data with verified contact information (e.g., emails).
- Packaging lead lists for sale.
- Marketing and selling lead lists through various channels.

## Lead Generation & Sales Workflow
1. **Target Market Definition:** Define specific criteria for target companies (industry, size, location).
2. **Data Collection (Scraping):** Utilize tools (if available) or manual methods to gather raw company data.
3. **Data Enrichment:** Use verification services or manual lookups to find and validate contact details (emails, LinkedIn profiles).
4. **Lead Packaging:** Format the leads into a deliverable (e.g., CSV file) with relevant information.
5. **Sales Channel Setup:**
    - IndieHackers: Post offers to the community.
    - Gumroad: Create product listings for automated sales.
    - Twitter: Direct outreach and promotional posts.
6. **Outreach & Promotion:** Actively market the lead lists to potential buyers.
7. **Delivery & Support:** Fulfill orders and provide customer support.

## Key Metrics
- Number of leads collected
- Number of verified emails
- Sales conversion rate
- Revenue generated per list

## Build Quality & Stability

### Uplift V2
- **Rock-solid stability** – Even at max height with 300 lbs, no wobble. The crossbar design distributes weight perfectly.
- **Heavy-duty frame** – Powder-coated steel, 3-stage legs lift smoothly every time.
- **Anti-collision sensor** – Stops on impact, programmable sensitivity.

User reviews (4.8/5 on Uplift site): \"No sway, even with triple monitors and speakers.\"

### Fully Jarvis
- **Elegant but slightly less stable** – Minor wobble at max height with heavy loads (user reports).
- **Lighter frame** – Still premium, but Uplift feels more industrial.
- **Anti-collision** – Standard on all models.

User reviews (4.7/5): \"Beautiful desk, but wish it was as tank-like as Uplift.\"

**Winner: Uplift** for raw stability.

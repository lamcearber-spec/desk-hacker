# Creator Distribution Playbook

*Source: [@LoicBerthelot](https://x.com/LoicBerthelot/status/2016180045656539353) — scaled Minea to $750K MRR, Dropmagic to $60K MRR*

## Core Idea

Skip paid ads early. Use small YouTube creators (2-10K subs) as your PMF lab — they work for commission, you learn what converts for $0 upfront.

**Why not paid ads?**
- Paid ads optimize for fast conversions but don't tell you if onboarding sucks, retention is broken, or if product solves a real problem
- You pay $100+ per signup only to learn 90% churn in week 1
- Ads amplify what works — they don't discover it

---

## 3-Tier Creator System

### Tier 1: Small Creators (2-10K followers)
**Purpose:** PMF discovery, script testing, zero risk

**Find them:**
- YouTube search: `[your category] + "tutorial"` → sort by upload date
- Look for: posted in last 30 days, consistent cadence, engaged comments
- 20 mins manual work or use tools like Blime

**Outreach:**
Record a 2-min Loom showing your face:
1. Compliment their specific recent video
2. Explain why your tool fits their audience
3. Demo the product
4. Offer 100% commission, no upfront
5. Promise upfront payment for video #2 if it works

**Deal:** 30-50% recurring revenue, affiliate links, zero risk both sides

**Call:** 15-30 min to learn their audience, walk through features, collaborate on script. Best creators internalize value and actually persuade (not just read scripts).

**What you learn:**
- Actual CAC
- Which messaging converts
- If people use the product after signup
- Objections (from comments)
- Retention past day 7

---

### Tier 2: Medium Creators (10-50K followers)
**Purpose:** Scale once economics proven

**When to move up:** CAC under $50, retention above 40%

**Deal options:**
1. Big upfront ($3-5K) + small commission (10-20%)
2. Small upfront ($1-2K) + big commission (40-50%)

**Pricing formula (140% breakeven):**
```
10K views → 100 signups → 20 paying customers → $1,580 MRR
Offer 70% of expected month 1 revenue upfront
Leave 30% margin for negotiation
```

**Outreach:** Lead with money. Include PROOF, URGENCY, and UPFRONT OFFER in Loom. Everyone gets 50 pitches/week.

---

### Tier 3: Big Creators (50K-100K+ followers)
**Purpose:** Scale to moon, unfair advantage

**When:** After $10K MRR

**Deal:** Quarterly contracts, multiple videos/month, $5-20K per video

**Math:** If creator generates $8K revenue month 1, pay $5K upfront = still profitable

**End goal:** Partner with a big creator, give equity, let them scale you

## Outreach Playbook

1. Make list of 50 creators under 10K subs
2. Record Loom template (customize first 20 seconds per creator)
3. Send first email with Loom link
4. Follow up on days 3, 7, 10, 14 with different angles
5. Hop on 15-min call to pitch partnership
6. Stay on them until video publishes (some ghost for 3 weeks — be annoying, it works)

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Using middlemen
- Tried at Minea, never worked
- If creator never speaks to you, they won't understand the vision
- Talk directly or don't do it

### ❌ Skipping small creators
- Bigger audience ≠ more signups
- You need to know economics and winning scripts first

### ❌ No creator-friendly funnel
- Don't paywall everything
- Give free "wow" moments
- Example: Dropmagic gives free AI Magic generator, charges to publish

## The Compound Effect

- Minea has 300+ creator videos on YouTube
- Each video generates passive signups daily
- One 18-month-old video drove 28 signups in January alone

**Progression:**
- Small creators → PMF
- Medium creators → $10K MRR
- Big creators → Unfair advantage
